About HI-PRAISE Table Water

HI-PRAISE Table Water is a progressive company that manufactures and distributes bottled and sachet drinking water that improves the quality of life. It is the leading packaged water production company in Warri and its environs, and is one of the pioneer brands in Nigeria.

The company continues to utilize emerging technology to develop new, innovative drinking water products and is committed to producing to the highest quality.

Utilizing state of the art purification, filtration and disinfection technologies, we produce safe and refreshing products with the best packaging options available. We are your number one source for clean and environmentally safe drinking water since 2004. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of pure, treated and properly packaged bottled and sachet table water, with an emphasis on quality, value, prestige and human satisfaction.

How We Got Started

The company was founded by Mrs Christy Esimobi in January 2004, was dully incorporated as an indigenous private limited liability company on 25th May 2004 with registration number RC123456, and commenced production in August 2004.

Our Mission

  1. Continue to provide top quality packaged water within its market zone such that the brand HI-PRAISE would become a household name in the table water supply business.
  2. To render efficient and unique service that would include household distribution of the dispenser bottled water.
  3. To generate employment and alleviate poverty in the economy.

The company in a bid to maintain its high quality standard improves and expands its facility on regular basis; this has earned her the leader in bottled water production in the oil rich city of Warri.

Quality Assurance

We maintain a quality assurance policy that is compliant with the stipulated regulations of NAFDAC, SON and all other regulatory bodies. Beyond this we view quality as part of the fabric of our entire system, and as such we drive a first-time-right alongside a continous improvement of culture. Quality is a mark of our brand and we are committed to entrenching it in every aspect of our operations.

Health, Safety and Environment (HSE)

HI-PRAISE aims to eliminate possible adverse effects of our operations on personnel and general populace. This is achieved through the creation of a culture that is adopted across board. Health, environmental protection and safety are important in the discharge of all day-to-day activities. By this we are able to eliminate unsafe working conditions and reduce our environmental impacts to as low as reasonably practicable.

Employee Relations

At HI-PRAISE Table Water we strive to create a work atmosphere that is professional, cordial and enjoyable. We ensure that our workers are adequately catered for and compensated accordingly. At HI-PRAISE Table Water , the welfare and wellbeing of our most valuable asset is of supreme importance to us: and as such, we are able to maintain a competent, motivated and happy workforce.
