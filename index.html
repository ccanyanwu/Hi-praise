About Us

Welcome to HI-PRAISE Table Water, your number one source for clean and environmentally safe drinking water since 2002. We're dedicated to providing you the very best of pure, treated and properly packaged table and sachet water, with an emphasis on quality, value, prestige and human satisfaction. Founded in 2002, HI-PRAISE Table Water has come a long way from its beginnings in a humble suburb in Warri. When we first started out, our passion for providing quality and safe drinking water to the people of Warri and beyond drove us to start this business which wasn't present at that time .We hope you enjoy our products as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.

Our Products

Presently we bottle clean and safe drinking water. Our fully automated plant ensures that there is no human interference during the production process. From bottle blowing to final packaging, the units are in an enclosed system which ensures there is no contamination with the final product. Our sterile filling process assures safety of products. As part of our commitment to help you enjoy life, we can personalize our products for your special occasions. These customized products will still bear our mark of safety and quality for the consumption of your guests and loved ones. We guarantee you peace of mind whenever you drink our water.
